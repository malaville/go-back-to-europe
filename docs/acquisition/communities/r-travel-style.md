# r/travel - Community Rules & Tone

> Fetched: 2026-03-08
> Source: [submission guidelines](https://www.reddit.com/r/travel/wiki/submission-guidelines/), [FAQ](https://www.reddit.com/r/travel/wiki/faq/), [subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/travel/)

## Rules

- **Search first**: Always check sub/FAQ before posting. Include specifics (dates, budget, research done) or posts get removed/downvoted.
- **Descriptive titles**: Vague queries like "flying question" fail. Include airports, airlines, citizenship for visa/flight topics.
- **No clickbait/promotion/politics**: Self-promo, surveys, rants, or off-topic debates lead to bans.
- **Research first**: Google/direct sources (airlines/embassies) before posting. Cite unclear findings.
- **No AI content**: Strictly banned, including rewrites or itineraries. Permanent ban.

## Tone & norms

- Advice without evidence or absolutes (e.g., "most routes work fine") draws downvotes as unsubstantiated.
- Users favor nuanced, sourced input.
- Airline topics redirect to r/flights or r/awardtravel.
- High-visibility comments amplify backlash if seen as opinionated.
