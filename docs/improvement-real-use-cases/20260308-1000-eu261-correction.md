## What happened
marcantow advised OP to invoke EU261 against Etihad for BKK→MUC cancellation. u/Available-Driver-791 questioned this.

## The correction

**EU261 does NOT apply to Etihad BKK→MUC.**

EU261 scope (Article 3(1)):
1. Departing FROM an EU airport → applies to ALL airlines
2. Arriving TO an EU airport from outside → applies ONLY to EU-based airlines

Etihad is UAE-based, departing from BKK (non-EU). Fails both conditions.

## What does apply
- Etihad's own conditions of carriage (rebooking/refund for cancellation)
- Montreal Convention (provable financial losses, not automatic compensation)
- If codeshare operated by EU carrier, could change (but unlikely here)

## Rule learned
**EU261 only protects on non-EU origin flights if the operating airline is EU-based.** Gulf carriers (Etihad, Emirates, Qatar) flying FROM outside the EU are NOT covered. Do not cite EU261 for these cases.
